About the Temple

Madurai Meenakshi Amman Temple is one of the most famous temples in India, located in Madurai, Tamil Nadu. The temple is dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi and Lord Sundaraeswar. It is known for its beautiful gopurams,sculpture and rich history.

Darshan Timings

  • Morning Darshan - 5:00 AM to 12:30 PM
  • Evening Darshan - 4:00 PM to 9:30 PM
  • Abhishekam - 6:00 AM
  • Night Pooja - 8:30 PM

Festivals

Chithirai Festival, Navaratri , Aadi Pooram, Theppam Festival and Thai Poosam are celebrated grandly every year at the temple.
